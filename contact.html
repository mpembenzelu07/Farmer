Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join SMART-FARMER COOP as a young innovator? +
We offer several youth programs including the AgriTech Fellowship, Startup Incubator, and Young Farmer Program. Visit our Youth Initiatives page to learn more about eligibility and application processes. Most programs accept applications quarterly.
Do you work with smallholder farmers directly? +
Yes, we work directly with smallholder farmer cooperatives and associations across Africa. Our technology solutions are co-designed with farmers to ensure they address real challenges and are accessible and affordable. Contact our farmer support team for more information.
How can my organization partner with SMART-FARMER COOP? +
We partner with universities, research institutions, NGOs, government agencies, and private companies. Partnership opportunities include technology co-development, research collaboration, implementation support, and funding. Please email partnerships@smartfarmercoop.africa with details about your organization and proposed collaboration.
Are your technology solutions available for purchase? +
Our solutions are available through different models: direct purchase, subscription services, and cooperative sharing arrangements. We offer flexible payment options including mobile money and payment plans. Contact our solutions team for pricing and availability in your region.
Do you offer training on using your agricultural technologies? +
Yes, we provide comprehensive training for all our technology solutions. Training includes hands-on workshops, digital tutorials, and ongoing support. We conduct training sessions at our regional offices, partner locations, and farmer field schools. Training costs are typically included in solution packages.
