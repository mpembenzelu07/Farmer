Youth Initiatives

Empowering Africa's next generation of agricultural innovators and entrepreneurs

Why Invest in Youth for Agriculture?

Africa has the world's youngest population, with 60% under 25 years old. At the same time, agriculture employs 60% of Africans. By connecting these two realities, we can transform Africa's largest sector while creating meaningful employment for millions of young people.

60% Of Africa's population is under 25
12M Youth enter African job market yearly
60% Of Africans work in agriculture
$1T Agribusiness potential by 2030

Our youth initiatives are designed to equip young Africans with the skills, technology, and opportunities to become leaders in agricultural innovation. We believe that Africa's youth are not just the future of agriculture - they are the present opportunity for transformation.
