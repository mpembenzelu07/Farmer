About SMART-FARMER COOP

Connecting Africa's educated youth with the continent's agricultural transformation

Our Story

Founded in 2018, SMART-FARMER COOP was born from a simple observation: Africa has the world's youngest population and the majority of its workforce in agriculture, yet these two realities rarely intersect meaningfully.

We recognized that while 60% of Africans work in agriculture, most educated youth were seeking opportunities outside the sector. At the same time, African agriculture was missing the innovation and technological advancement needed to reach its full potential.

Our mission became clear: create a bridge between Africa's educated youth and its agricultural sector. We believe that by empowering young Africans with the tools, knowledge, and platforms to innovate in agriculture, we can transform the continent's largest employer into its most dynamic economic engine.

Today, we operate across 12 African countries, working with over 50,000 young innovators and 200,000 smallholder farmers to create sustainable, technology-driven agricultural solutions.

Our Mission

To empower Africa's educated youth to develop and implement innovative, technology-driven solutions that transform traditional agriculture into a sustainable, profitable, and modern sector that drives continental development and creates meaningful employment for millions.

Our Vision

Africa as the world's breadbasket, where agriculture is a dynamic, technology-driven sector led by young innovators, providing food security, economic prosperity, and sustainable livelihoods for all Africans.
